Privacy Policy for MyDeenIslam

Effective Date: Feb 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyDeenIslam ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using MyDeenIslam, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

MyDeenIslam requires certain device permissions to function correctly. We only access this data to deliver specific app features locally on your device. We do not collect, store on external servers, or share your personal data.

  • Location Services (Coarse and Fine Location): We request location access solely to calculate accurate prayer times (Salah) and to determine the correct Qibla direction based on your current geographical position. This data is processed locally on your device to fetch accurate timings and is never tracked or shared.
  • Microphone and Audio Settings (Record Audio / Modify Audio): Our application includes a technical library dependency that requests microphone and audio permissions. Please note: MyDeenIslam does not actively use your microphone. We do not record, listen to, collect, store, or transmit any audio data or conversations.
  • Storage (Read and Write External Storage): We request storage access to allow the app to save downloaded content (such as offline resources or personalized settings) directly to your device.
  • Notifications and Alarms (Post Notifications, Vibrate, System Alert Window): To ensure you receive timely prayer alerts, we use these permissions to send push notifications, trigger vibrations, and display Adhan (call to prayer) alerts over other apps at the correct times.
  • Internet Access: The app requires an internet connection to fetch updated prayer times and stream content.

2. No Third-Party Services or Tracking

MyDeenIslam does not use any third-party analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. All core functionality is designed to respect your privacy and keep your data on your device.

3. Data Security

We value your trust. Because we do not collect or store your personal data on external servers, your information remains as secure as the device you use the app on.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

5. Links to External Sites

This app may contain links to other educational or religious sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we strongly advise you to review their specific Privacy Policies.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
